ABOUT ME

I enjoy creating seamless and impactful user and client experiences through research, design, and words. I currently live in Kenya, but I have had the privilege to work with different teams in Ghana, Nigeria, Rwanda, and South Africa. I have also worked in various sectors, notably-Health, entertainment, Agriculture, Transport, Fintech, and Finance.

Previously, I worked in banking as a teller and later on as a Priority and Business Clients service manager. During my time as a client-facing banker, I learned about the success any organization can reap by focusing on excellent client service and experience. Later on, when I joined the Meltwater Entrepreneurial School of Technology in 2018, I saw an opportunity to merge the client service experience gained from working at the bank with design, understanding the power of quantitative and qualitative research and human-computer interactions to become a UX/UI designer.

Understanding why people do what they do or act in a particular way is essential to creating impactful products. I love using my skill set to create experiences that make life easier.
